What is the isoelectric point?
The isoelectirc point is the PH at which a particular molecule carries no net electrical charge. The net charge on the molecule is a ffected by the pH of it's surrounding environment and can become more negative or positive due to the gain or loss of protons.

Why use isoelectric point?
